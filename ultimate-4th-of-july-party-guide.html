Planning Your Patriotic Party

Whether you're celebrating the 4th of July, Memorial Day, or any USA-themed event, the right patriotic party supplies make all the difference. Here's how to pull it together without stress.

Start with a Color Scheme

Red, white, and blue is the obvious choice — but the key is balance. Too much of any one color looks flat. Mix textures: solid red tablecloths with white star-print napkins and blue balloon clusters work well together.

Layer Your Decorations

Think in three zones: overhead (banners, streamers, balloons), table level (centerpieces, tableware, confetti), and ground level (pinwheels, flags, pathway markers). Layering creates depth and makes the space feel intentional.

Don't Forget the Table Setup

A well-set table anchors the whole party. Use a patriotic table cover as your base, then build up with themed plates, cups, and napkins. Add a centerpiece — a simple flag arrangement or a star-shaped balloon cluster works great.

Timing and Setup

Set up decorations the day before if possible. Balloons can be inflated the morning of the event. Outdoor decorations should be secured — wind is the enemy of streamers and banners.
